## Ingredients

### For the berry compote
- 150g raspberries  
- 150g blueberries  
- 1 tbsp caster sugar (or maple syrup)

### For the oats
- 80g porridge oats  
- 125ml Alpro No Sugars Oat non-dairy drink  
- 100g Alpro Vanilla (or plain) non-dairy yogurt alternative  
- Â¼ tsp ground cinnamon  
- 1 tbsp chia seeds  

### To serve
- 2 tbsp toasted flaked almonds

## Method

### 1. Prepare the night before
Put **125g raspberries** and **125g blueberries** into a small saucepan with the sugar (or maple syrup).  
Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for **3â€“4 minutes**, until the fruit softens slightly.  
Leave to cool, then transfer to a sealable container and chill overnight.

In a separate sealable container, mix together the oats, oat drink, non-dairy yogurt, cinnamon, and chia seeds.  
Stir well, cover, and chill overnight.

### 2. Assemble in the morning
Layer alternating spoonfuls of the oats and berry compote into **two glasses or jars**.  
If the oats are very thick, loosen with a splash of extra oat drink.  
Sprinkle over some toasted almonds as you layer.

Top with the **reserved berries** and a final sprinkle of almonds before serving.
